Hello. I am an Economist at the Global Development Network and a PhD candidate in Development Economics at the Geneva Graduate Institute. My research focuses on digital economics, migration, public finance, gender, and household behaviour in developing countries. I am also a research fellow at the International Institute of Migration and Development and I hold a Master in Development Studies from the Graduate Institute and a Bachelor in Economics from the University of Delhi.
