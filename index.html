About

Balu is a third‑year PhD candidate in Development Economics at the Geneva Graduate Institute. He is a research fellow at the International Institute of Migration and Development and previously worked as a research associate at the Indian School of Business. His research focuses on migration, public finance and household behaviour in developing countries. Balu holds a Master in Development Studies from the Graduate Institute and a Bachelor in Economics from the University of Delhi.

Research Interests

  • Applied Microeconomics
  • Public Health
  • Migration
  • Public Finance
